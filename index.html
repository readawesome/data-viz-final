Political Tweets Classifier

This is a platform that educates voters on different candidates, and their stances on popular policies.
These are then represented in a line plot for voters to understand the policies that are emphasized by different political figures prior to the upcoming 2020 presidential election. The goal of this classifier is to inform users about the goals and priorities in relation to public policy of the political candidates.
